What are Ideal Gasses?

Ideal gasses are real gasses that follow Kinetic Molecular Theory (KMT)

  1. Gases consist of small particles, spaced far apart
  2. Collisions between are elastic â€“ meaning no energy is lost during these collisions
  3. Gas particles are in continuous, straight line motion
  4. No attraction or repulsion between particles
  5. Kinetic energy is directly related to temperature

Ideal Gas Formula: PV=nRT. You can find P(pressure), V(volume), n(moles), T(temperature) of a gas by knowing the other 3 characteristic. R is the ideal gas law constant in order to connect all the units together.
